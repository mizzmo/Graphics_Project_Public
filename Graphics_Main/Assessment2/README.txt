Controls:

GENERAL:
SPACEBAR: Change Active Camera.
ESC: Close Scene.
Drag Window: Resize Scene.
E: Reset UFO afer it has dissapeared.

MODEL-VIEWER CAM:
ARROW KEYS: Move Model-Viewer Camera.
R and F: Zoom In / Our Model-Viewer Camera.

FIRST PERSON CAM:
Mouse Movement: Move camera angle in first person camera.
WASD: Move around the scene in first person camera.
Mouse Click (On UFO): Interact with the UFO to make it fly away.

ROTATING CAM:
Left and Right Bracket: Increase / Decrease speed of rotating camera.
